Little Navmap

News

2016-12-18

  • Little Navmap beta version 1.2.0 released
    Download LittleNavmap-1.2.0.beta.zip for Windows
    Download LittleNavmap-1.2.0.beta.dmg for macOS
    New: Printing for map, flight plan, airport and weather information added.
    Weather is now shown in decoded format.
    AI and multiplayer aircraft are now shown on the map. Information about these is available in the aircraft progress window and tooltips.
    Added configurable top of descent display on map.
    Little Navmap can now connect to a flight simulator directly.
    Flight plans can now be exported to the GFP format used by the Flight1 GTN 650/750.
    Many more improvements and bug fixes.
